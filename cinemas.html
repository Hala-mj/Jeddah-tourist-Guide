MUVI Cinema

is a Saudi Etisalat Cinema chain, and is one of the local cinema display brands in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Riyadh, the company is owned and operated by Muvi Cinema Company. VOX Cinemas

is a chain of theater and cinema halls operating in the Middle East and managed by the Majid Al Futtaim Group, headquartered in the United Arab Emirates. Majid Al Futtaim has 129 cinema screens across the UAE, Qatar, Lebanon, Egypt, Oman, Bahrain and Saudi Arabia under the VOX Cinemas brand.
