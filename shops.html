Red Sea Mall

Redsea Mall is a shopping center located in the north of Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. The center specifically is located west of King Abdulaziz Road and includes 18 entrances and a parking lot. A mall, a games city, and many international and local shops. The center is distinguished from other centers in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia by having the largest indoor fountain and the largest glass roof

Arab mall

Mall of Arabia is located 10 minutes from the airport. The mall offers visitors a wide range of shops, ranging from international to local brands, and includes about 408 malls. It is one of the favorite destinations for families and individuals, whether for entertainment or shopping. It extends over a large area, approximately 261,000 square meters, and to provide comfort and safety for its visitors, about 3,000 parking spaces have been allocated.
