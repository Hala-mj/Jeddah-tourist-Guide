Billy Beez

The Billy Beez chain is one of the largest entertainment amusement parks in the world. It was established in New York and its branches expanded throughout Europe, the Middle East and Africa. The first branch was opened in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in 2011 in Jeddah in Haifa Mall, which is characterized by the presence of many fun games and various adventures.
