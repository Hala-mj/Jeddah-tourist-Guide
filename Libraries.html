King Fahd Public Library

The King Fahd Public Library in Jeddah is considered the best public library in the Mecca region, and also one of the best public libraries in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the Middle East. It operates consistently to provide all cultural, scientific, and entertainment aspects to all segments of society without discrimination. It plays an active and vital role in the community, providing constructive and effective participation.
