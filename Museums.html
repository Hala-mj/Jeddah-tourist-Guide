Hayy Jameel

Hayy Jameel, Art Jameel's new center in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, is a 17,000-square-meter creative hub designed for dynamic arts and community engagement. Named after the Arabic word for neighborhood, it signifies accessibility and collaboration. The center reflects Art Jameel's commitment to supporting artists, creative communities, and entrepreneurship in Saudi Arabia and beyond.

Athr Gallery

Athr Gallery has played a pivotal role in building the Contemporary art scene in Saudi; valuing experimentation and the exploration of new ideas, disseminating the artists’ role in the creative process and the impact they can have on societies.

Al-Tayebat International Museum

The museum consists of several pavilions that vary according to the collectibles displayed in it. The most important are the Qur’an, manuscripts, and Islamic artifacts, in addition to the wonderful heritage wall paintings.

Location

House of Islamic Arts

House of Islamic arts is the first museum of its kind in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, as a museum specialized in Islamic arts. From the far countries of China and India to the farthest countries of the Maghreb and Andalusia of various arts, where the museum contains more than a thousand pieces of art representing 15 centuries of the history of Islamic art from all countries of the Islamic world.

Location

Khuzam Palace

Among the most luxurious museums in Jeddah and one of the high-end royal palaces that were inhabited by King Abdulaziz, and a bureau to receive VIPs and officials, and after the death of King Abdulaziz, the palace turned into a museum.

Location

Museum of Arts Home

The Museum of Home Arts is specialized in displaying home art and how to show creativity in coordinating collectibles and colors. It contains a collection of ancient artifacts that are more than 70 years old. Its tourist value is that it complies with the requirements of the tourist market at the present time, as it gives an idea of the way to arrange houses and palaces in different societies.
